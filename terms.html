2. Service Description

Trade Escalator is a trading alert escalation and notification management tool. The Service provides:

  • Multi-tier alert escalation through push notifications, SMS messages, and phone calls until you acknowledge the alert.
  • Customizable escalation rules based on symbol matching, timeframes, alert tiers, direction filters, and trading session windows.
  • Integration with thinkorswim and other trading platforms via deep links, allowing you to open your broker's app directly from an alert.
  • Webhook-based acknowledgment notifications sent to Discord, Slack, or custom endpoints you configure.
  • Analytics including acknowledgment rates, response time metrics, and alert volume by symbol.
  • Quiet hours settings to control when escalations are active.

What Trade Escalator Is Not: The Service is not a brokerage, trading platform, investment advisor, or financial service. We do not execute trades, access brokerage accounts, manage portfolios, or provide financial advice of any kind.
